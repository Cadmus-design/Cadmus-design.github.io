Chunghwa Telecom Singapore Pte Ltd

The CHT-SG was established in September 2008. We are the one of Chunghwa Telecom’s (http://www.cht.com.tw) oversea subsidiaries. Our head quarter is at Singapore. Obtains the Facilities-Based Operator (FBO) license from the Infocomm Development Authority of Singapore. Besides the distribution of CHT Taiwan’s ASEAN business, also aggressive to explore the ASEAN region market. Launched enterprise communication solutions, communication products and multimedia broadband value-added services to corporate and individual users.
  • SEP 2008, Company Established
  • OCT 2008, Obtain FBO license from Singapore IDA.
  • MAY 2009, Established the 1st POP at Equinix Singapore.
  • JUN 2009, Established the Voice Switch POP at Equinix Singapore.
  • SEP 2009, Signing the ST-2 satellite joint venture with Singtel.
  • DEC 2010, Established the 2nd POP at Global Switch Singapore.
  • MAY 2011, the ST-2 satellite successfully launched.
  • JUL 2016, Completed the South East Asia (10x 10G) Ring Networks through Hong Kong, Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, Malaysia and Singapore).
