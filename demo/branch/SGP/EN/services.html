South-East Asia Japan Cable System (SJC)

  • The South-East Asia Japan Cable System (SJC) is a new generation 8900 km submarine cable system connecting 7 Asian countries and regions including Brunei, Mainland China, Hong Kong, Japan, Singapore, the Philippines and Thailand. RFS date on June 27, 2013, the SJC cable consists of 6 fiber pairs, with design capacity of 45.5 Tbps. It utilizes the state-of-the-art advanced 100G SLTE and OADM Branching technologies.

South-East Asia Japan 2 Cable System (SJC2)

  • The South-East Asia Japan 2 Cable System (SJC2) is a new generation 10500 km submarine cable system connecting Singapore, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Hong Kong, Taiwan, mainland China, Korea and Japan. It has 7 fiber pairs and a design capacity of 126 Terabits per second. The system is expected to be completed by the fourth quarter of 2020
