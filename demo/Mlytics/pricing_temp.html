FAQ

Do I need to sign contracts with each CDN provider?

No, you do not need to enter a contract agreement or sign anything for all CDNs listed on the Mlytics platform. If applicable, you could also bring the CDN youre currently using to the platform for enhanced traffic management.

What is Mlytics Free credit?

Mlytics Free credit is offered together with the Free tier so users can explore and evaluate the platforms products and services free of charge. The Free credit gives users a USD 300-worth of usable credit for 30 days. Learn more about Mlytics Free program here.

What is Mlytics Free tier?

Mlytics Free tier is also offered together with the Free credit so users can explore and evaluate the platforms products and services free of charge. The Free tier is limited free access to many common Mlytics products and services. Learn more about Mlytics Free tier here.

How can I be eligible for Mlytics Free credit and Free tier?

All users who have a valid credit card and successfully added a site to the platform are entitled to the Free credit and Free tier. Learn more about Free credit here, and Free tier here

Are the Free credit and Free tier available for the Developer and Business plan?

Yes. Both Developer and Business plan users are entitled to the Mlytics Free credit and Free tier. Users of both plans can avail the Free credit and Free tier to explore and evaluate Mlytics products and services. Learn more about Free credit here, and Free tier here

Which CDNs are available on each plan?

The Developer Plan includes Basic CDN, Global CDN, and Regional CDN. The Business plan includes all CDNs included in the Developer plan plus China RIM CDN. Enterprise plan includes all CDNs included in the Business plan plus China CDN. Learn more about all CDNs offered on the Mlytics marketplace here.

How is the traffic pricing calculated for the Basic CDN?

All traffic generated via Basic CDN (Cloudfront, GMA, and Stackpath) is calculated in one pool. Once the pooled traffic exceeded the Free tier quota, additional pooled traffic will be billed. Check the pricing page for more details.

How is the traffic pricing calculated for the CDNs other than the Basic CDN?

Traffic generated via CDN other than the Basic CDN is calculated and billed individually. Check the pricing page for more details.

Can I cancel my subscription whenever I want?

For Developer and Business plan users, subscription fees are charged monthly, and subscriptions are auto-renewed every month. You can remove your site from the platform and cancel at any time. For Enterprise users, all subscriptions are annually-renewed, and please contact our Sales to cancel your subscription.

How does a credit limit work?

The credit limit is the maximum amount of debt that the user is allowed to borrow when using Mlytics products and services. Each plan has a different credit limit. Exceeding the credit limit will trigger billing. Learn more about the Mlytics credit limit here.

Does my plan already include Origin Shield?

Origin Shield is an Add-on service. Both Developer and Business plans need to purchase Origin Shield Add-on first in order to activate Origin Shield. Learn more about the activating Origin Shield here.
